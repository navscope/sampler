About TEDx: Paradox of Thoughts

Join us for an extraordinary exploration of life's most intriguing contradictions. This independently organized TED event brings together philosophers, scientists, artists, and thought leaders to unravel the paradoxes that define our existence. From quantum mechanics to human consciousness, we'll dive deep into the beautiful complexity of opposing truths that somehow coexist.
